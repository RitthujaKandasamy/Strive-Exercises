Hi, I am Ritthuja

Data Scientist

I am from Tamilnadu, India. I did Mathematics as my majore subject. I love to take nature photos. I have worked as a primary school teacher for two years in India, teaching for grades 1 and 2. After that, managing and teaching children became an easy task for me. I came to Germany after I married my husband. At that time, marriage was my only reason for coming to Germany. But since our parents arranged our marriage, leaving my comfort zone behind to live in an unknown country with a stranger was scary. Yet, that did not stop me from putting myself in challenging situations so that I could improve. I went to supermarkets, government offices, etc., alone. I also started taking the German language course at the VHS. Two and half years have passed since then. Now, Germany is no more an unknown country to me; it is the place I call home.
