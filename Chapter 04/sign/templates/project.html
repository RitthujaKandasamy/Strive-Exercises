What can Signa do for you?

Signa can help break your boundaries, broaden your horizon, and make friends. Now you can talk with people just by using ASL. All you have to do is, click on the 'Start Talking' button below. It will open your webcam in a small window. Once your webcam has started, start talking to your friend in sign langage. Our app will start translating your actions into words, which will be displayed below the webcam window. At the same time, the translated text will also be spoken out using AI voices so that your friend can easily understand you.
